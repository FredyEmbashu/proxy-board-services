Our Story

Proxy Group was established in 2015 with a vision to transform board governance in Namibia. Recognizing the need for specialized board services in a rapidly evolving corporate landscape, our founders brought together expertise in governance, secretarial services, and technology to create a company dedicated to enhancing board effectiveness.

Today, Proxy Group continues to innovate and expand our service offerings, integrating emerging technologies like AI and data analytics to provide cutting-edge solutions for modern boards. Our dedication to excellence and continuous improvement ensures that we remain at the forefront of board services in Namibia and beyond.

Our Approach to Board Services

How we deliver exceptional value to our clients

Diagnostic Assessment

We begin every client relationship with a comprehensive diagnostic assessment of current board practices, identifying strengths, gaps, and opportunities for enhancement.

Data-Driven Decision Making

Leveraging our expertise in data science and analytics, we provide boards with actionable insights based on rigorous analysis and advanced statistical methodologies.

AI-Enhanced Solutions

We incorporate cutting-edge artificial intelligence to provide boards with predictive analytics, pattern recognition, and intelligent automation for improved governance.

Behavioral Economics Perspective

Drawing on behavioral economics principles, we help boards understand decision-making biases and implement frameworks that lead to more rational and effective governance.

Customized Solutions

Based on our assessment findings, we develop tailored solutions that align with your organization's unique governance needs, culture, and strategic objectives.

Implementation & Support

We don't just recommend solutions—we help implement them. Our team provides hands-on support throughout the implementation process with measurable outcomes.
